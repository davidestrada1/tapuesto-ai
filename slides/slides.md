# tapuesto.ai вҖ” The Prediction Market for People

**Where social betting meets AI-powered reputation**

---

## The Problem

People love:
- рҹҸҶ Showing off their expertise
- рҹ’° Making friendly bets with stakes
- рҹ“Ҡ Comparing their knowledge vs. peers

**Problem**: No fun, gamified way to track prediction track records, build reputation, and compare with friends.

---

## The Solution

**tapuesto.ai** вҖ” a social prediction market where you:

- Create bets on anything (*"Who will lose the most weight?"*)
- Wager money, drinks, or bragging rights
- Earn **ELO rating** like a chess grandmaster
- Build a **prediction credit score**
- Compare your accuracy vs. friends and strangers
- Become known as "the expert" in any topic

## Gamification & Reputation

- **ELO Rating** вҖ” start at 1200, climb as you predict correctly
- **Leaderboard** вҖ” 3 tiers: Bronze, Silver, Grandmaster
- **Win Rate** вҖ” percentage of correct predictions
- **Active Streaks** вҖ” consistent predictors get visibility
- **"Payer Score"** вҖ” reliability when you lose (do you pay up?)
